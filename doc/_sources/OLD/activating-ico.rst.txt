
Activating your Tezos ICO Tokens
================================

For people who participated to Tezos fundraiser, and who did not
activate their Tezos wallet before (Tezos) block 600,000, they can still
activate their account on Dune after airdrop.

Using Dune Web wallet
~~~~~~~~~~~~~~~~~~~~~

The steps to activate your fundraiser account using Dune Web wallet are detailed
in `this section <using-a-wallet.html#restoring-an-existing-account>`__.

Using Comannd-line client
~~~~~~~~~~~~~~~~~~~~~~~~~

The steps to activate your fundraiser account using ``dune-client`` command-line
are detailed in `this section <using-a-wallet.html#id3>`__.
